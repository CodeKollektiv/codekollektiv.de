Special iOS 5 Tech Talk Cocoaheads Meetup on Nov 2

The Apple iOS 5 Tech Talk World Tour will be in Berlin on November 2. Since so many iOS devs will be in town that day, we would like to invite you to a special Cocoaheads Berlin Tech Talk Meetup hosted at CodeKollektiv. We will start at 19:30 on November 2 after the Tech Talk has finished. You're all welcome, whether you attended the Tech Talk or not.

To find us after the Tech Talk, either take the subway line U6 from Friedrichstraße (where the Maritim Pro Arte hotel is) towards Alt-Mariendorf and exit at Mehringdamm. From there, it's a 5-10 minute walk (see below). Or share a taxi with others for the 3 km drive (shouldn't cost more than 8-10 EUR).

What is CodeKollektiv?

We are a group of 8 (at last count) indie/freelance iOS and Mac developers.
The CodeKollektiv is the office that we share together in Berlin.

We host Cocoaheads Berlin

We are proud hosts of the monthly Cocoaheads Berlin meetups.
Join us on every third Wednesday of the month, starting at 19:00.

Are you looking for iOS or Mac developers?

Many of us do client projects as freelancers, either alone or in a team. If you are looking for one or more experienced iOS or Mac OS X developers, please write to info@codekollektiv.de. Please include as much information as possible about yourself and the project you have in mind.

Note: We read every e-mail. If you do not hear from one of us after a few days, it means that we are currently all busy with other work or not interested in your project.
